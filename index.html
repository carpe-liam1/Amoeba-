About Amoeba

Amoeba is an interactive text distortion tool. Type your text in the input above and manipulate the text with right clicking your mouse. The slider above allows you to adjust the size of your distortion. Click undo once to have the text shift back to its original position. Click undo again to pause the shift back. Save to download your distorted text as a PNG image.
